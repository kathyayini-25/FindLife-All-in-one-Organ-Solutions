About Us

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every person in need of an organ transplant has access to a lifesaving transplant in a timely and equitable manner.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate the public and healthcare professionals about the importance of organ donation, to promote organ donation as a lifesaving act, and to support families of organ donors and transplant recipients through the organ transplantation process.

Our Services

We provide a range of services to support organ donation and transplantation, including:

  • Public education and awareness campaigns
  • Training and support for healthcare professionals
  • Support and guidance for families of organ donors and transplant recipients
  • Coordination of organ transplantation and matching between donors and recipients

Impact

Since our inception, Mohan Foundation has helped thousands of people in need of an organ transplant. We have successfully facilitated numerous organ transplantations, bringing hope and new life to many families.
